Tell the Tale (Formally known as Kill, Loot, Craft)

  • High Concept: Survive in the mansion
  • Player Experience Goals: The play should feel tense and mildly disturbed.
  • Genre: Survival
  • Game play: The player must not die and get out of the mansion
  • Prototype goal: Multiple Features
  • Features: You get to explore, pick up keys, and collect items
  • Setting/Premise: Mansion
  • Story: You awake in the middle of the night upon the dirty brick floors of a dungeon. You do not know how you got there but one thing is certain, you have to get out.
  • Target Audience: People who enjoy survival games.
  • Play time: 10 minutes.
  • Strategies: Do not engage enemies.
